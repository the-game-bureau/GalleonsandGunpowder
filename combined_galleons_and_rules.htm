âš“ Optional Rules for Extra Swashbuckling Fun

  • Treasure Chest: Each player may hide a single treasure chest (one square) on their grid. If hit, the attacker loses their next turn and must shout, "Ye found me booty, but no rum for ye this turn!"
  • Hidden Fog: Each player can declare one 2x2 section of their Sea Map as "fogged." The attacker must guess twice to confirm a hit in this area.
  • The Kraken Strikes: Once per game, each player can declare "Kraken!" and choose a 3x3 area of the opponent's Sea Map. The opponent must reveal whether any part of a ship is in that area, but no ship takes damage.
  • Buried Treasure: Each player can designate one square as "buried treasure." If the opponent hits it, they lose their next two turns as their crew digs for gold.
  • Sabotage!: Each player gets one chance per game to sabotage the enemy by calling out a coordinate where no shot is fired. Instead, the opponent must declare if they have a ship adjacent to that spot.
  • Shifting Tides: Before the game begins, each player may secretly "move" one ship one square in any direction without overlapping. This move is revealed only when a shot hits the ship in its new position.
