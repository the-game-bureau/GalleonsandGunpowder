Rules

Hoist the Sails for Adventure

Before you begin, ensure you have:

  • A worthy opponent.
  • A printed Skirmish Tracker.
  • A pencil, pen, or highlighter.
  • A thirst for adventure!

Glory or Oblivion!

Your mission is to sink all your opponent's ships before they sink yours!

  • Use a Skirmish Tracker to chart your cannon fire and track your Armada's survival.
  • Print two Skirmish Trackers: one for you, and one for your opponent.

☠ The Armada

Each captain commands an Armada of five ships:

  • Galleon (5 squares): The mightiest ship.
  • Brigantine (4 squares): A sturdy and swift vessel.
  • Frigate (3 squares): A versatile warship.
  • Sloop (3 squares): Quick and reliable.
  • Cutter (2 squares): The nimblest ship.

Gameplay

Taking Turns

Decide who fires the first cannonball by flipping a coin, tossing a gold doubloon, or shouting “Arrr!” the loudest. The loudest pirate takes the first shot!

Fire Your Cannons

  1. Announce a coordinate (e.g., "D6").
  2. Your opponent checks their Sea Map:
    • If you hit a ship: they’ll cry out, “Arrrrrrrgh! Ye’ve struck true!”
    • If you miss: they’ll jeer, “Ha! Ye missed, ye scallywag!”

Track Your Shots

Mark hits with an “X” and misses by shading or filling the circle on your Skirmish Tracker.

Sink a Ship

When all squares of a ship are hit, your opponent must declare, “You sunk my [ship name]!” (e.g., "You sunk my Cutter!").

💰 Claiming the Treasure

The loser, alas, must walk the plank and join their shipmates in Davy Jones' Locker! Declare their defeat with a hearty:

“Overboard ye go! May the sharks sing tales of yer folly as ye descend to Davy Jones' Locker!”

⚓ Optional Rules for Extra Swashbuckling Fun

  • Treasure Chest: Hide a treasure chest (1 square). If hit, the attacker loses their next turn and must shout, "Ye found me booty, but no rum for ye this turn!"
  • Hidden Fog: Declare one 2x2 section of your Sea Map as "fogged." The attacker must guess twice to confirm a hit in this area.
  • The Kraken Strikes: Once per game, declare "Kraken!" and choose a 3x3 area of your opponent’s Sea Map. They must reveal if any ship is there, but no ship takes damage.
  • Buried Treasure: Designate one square as "buried treasure." If hit, the attacker loses their next two turns.
  • Sabotage!: Call out a coordinate to disrupt your opponent by forcing them to declare if a ship is adjacent to the spot.
  • Shifting Tides: Before the game begins, secretly move one ship one square in any direction. This move is revealed only when the ship is hit.
