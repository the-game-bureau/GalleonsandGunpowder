Galleons and Gunpowder

by TheGameBureau.com

ALL HANDS ON DECK!

Hoist the sails, grab yer cutlass, and dive into a rip-roarin’ adventure on the high seas! Arrr! Prepare yerself, captain, for a swashbucklin’ quest where strategy, wit, and daring will decide the victor. Take up yer place on the High Seas, chart yer course, and let the cannonballs fly. The call to adventure be upon ye—do ye have what it takes to rule the waves?

PREPARE FOR GLORY OR OBLIVION!

Your mission is to sink all your opponent's ships before they sink yours!

ARMAMENTS O’ THE HIGH SEAS

Ye’ll be needin’ the followin’ to embark on yer grand skirmish:

  • 2 Skirmish Trackers — one fer chartin’ yer cannon fire and the survival o’ yer mighty Armada, and the other fer yer worthy adversary.
  • 2 quills or other scribe’s tools fer markin’ the fate o’ the battle.

YER ARMADA

Five ships await you Captain:

Galleon (5 squares): The mightiest ship.
Brigantine (4 squares): A sturdy and swift vessel.
Frigate (3 squares): A versatile warship.
Sloop (3 squares): Quick and reliable.
Cutter (2 squares): The nimblest ship.

But alas, matey, your opponent has an identical Armada as well!

PREPARE FER BATTLE!

In secret, lay yer ships upon The High Seas Grid at the top o' the Skirmish Tracker. Use yer quill or some other scribe’s tool to mark the outline o’ each vessel. Ships can be placed either horizontally or vertically, but beware! Take care not to place 'em diagonally nor let 'em drift off the edge o' the map, ye scurvy dog!

THE HUSH BEFORE THE BROADSIDE!

Decide who fires the first thunderin' cannonball by flippin' a shiny gold doubloon, ye salty sea dogs!

BEHOLD THE SKIRMISH TRACKER!

TO ARMS! THE SKIRMISH BE UPON US!

The leadin’ captain lets loose their cannons with a thunderous bellow, "Fire at D6, ye bilge rats!", for example.
The unfortunate buccaneer, with a wicked grin, checks their Skirmish Tracker.
HIT: If a ship takes a blow, they’ll roar, "Arrrrrrrgh! Ye’ve struck true, ye sea devil!"
MISS: But if it’s a miss, they’ll cackle, "Har har! Ye missed, ye scurvy scallywag!"
The captain marks their triumph with an X on the spot or their folly by shadin’ the circle with their scribe’s tool.
When all squares of a ship be blasted to the depths, the unfortunate buccaneer must cry out, "Ye’ve sent me [ship name] to Davy Jones’ locker!" (e.g., "Ye’ve sunk me Cutter!").

GLORY AND OBLIVION

But the victor, bold and cunning, claims the treasure and the glory! Let their triumph ring across the high seas with a proud declaration: "Huzzah! The spoils be mine! Raise a tankard in my honor, ye scurvy dogs, for I am the ruler o’ these waters!"
The defeated buccaneer must, alas, walk the plank and join their shipmates in Davy Jones’ Locker! Let their defeat be declared with a thunderous cry: "Overboard ye go! May the sharks weave tales o’ yer folly as ye sink to the depths o’ Davy Jones’ domain!"

DEAD MAN'S VOLLEY

Optional Rule: When a pirate captain be down to their final ship, they unleash the fury o’ the seas in a desperate act o’ defiance! With no quarter left to give, they fire not one, but two cannonballs in a single volley, aiming to send their foes to the briny deep.

This mighty broadside strikes fear into even the boldest adversaries, for a cornered captain fights with the wrath of a thousand storms! Beware, ye scallywags—when the last ship sails, it sails with vengeance in its wake!

GO FORTH AND CONQUER!

May the winds guide yer cannonballs true, and may the waters run red with the defeat of yer foes! Grab yer cutlass and stoke yer thirst for plunder as ye set forth to etch yer name in the legends of the high seas. Prove yerself the fiercest buccaneer to ever command the waves. The glory of the seas be yers for the takin’—Arrr!
